ABOUT US

Weather refers to the atmospheric conditions prevailing in a specific area at a given time. It encompasses a range of elements, including temperature, humidity, precipitation, wind speed and direction, and atmospheric pressure. Weather conditions are influenced by various factors, including geographic location, altitude, proximity to water bodies, and the Earth's rotation.The study of weather is known as meteorology, and it plays a crucial role in understanding and predicting climate patterns. Meteorologists use advanced technology such as satellites, weather radars, and computer models to gather data and make accurate forecasts. Weather impacts daily life, agriculture, transportation, and various industries.Different weather phenomena, such as rain, snow, thunderstorms, and hurricanes, result from complex interactions within the Earth's atmosphere. Seasonal changes, driven by the tilt of the Earth's axis, bring about variations in weather patterns throughout the year.People often rely on weather forecasts to plan activities, make informed decisions, and stay prepared for potential hazards.
